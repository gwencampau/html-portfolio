Best Movies According to Gwen

My Current Top 3

The Incredibles

This movie was such a big part of my childhood. I loved it then and still love it now. It's just the right amount of drama and comedy.

Austenland

My favorite spin-off of Pride and Prejudice. It's very funny and made by the same people as Napoleon Dynamite.

Secondhand Lions

Heart-warming story-within-a-story type of movie. Contains many shenanigans involving grumpy old men and a lion.
